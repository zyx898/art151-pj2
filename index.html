Game of Crypto - ShfitCoin

Game Rule

You will have 500$ in your saving when you start the game. Your goal is to make decisions with your own money and see if you can manage your money in a good way so you don't go homeless.

This game will weekly Investment game. Every week you have a chances to decide if you want to invtest your money in Crypto, sell your crypto, or hold your crypto. You can choose to descover other coin as well.

Every week you will need 300$ or more in your saving account for you not going homeless.
Random event will occurs in the weekly bases which will cost you money.
Every week you will get 500$ for working 6 day part time job.
